About Cristiano

Cristiano Ronaldo is a Portuguese professional footballer, widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time. Born on February 5, 1985, in Madeira, Portugal, he began his senior career with Sporting CP before moving to Manchester United in 2003. He later played for Real Madrid, Juventus, and returned to Manchester United before joining Al Nassr in Saudi Arabia.

Ronaldo has won numerous titles, including multiple UEFA Champions League trophies, league titles in England, Spain, and Italy, and the 2016 UEFA European Championship with Portugal. Known for his incredible goal-scoring ability, athleticism, and work ethic, he has won five Ballon dOr awards. Off the field, he is a global icon with a massive social media following and numerous endorsement deals.
